What is "Wear You Wander?"

Wear You Wander is an application that analyzes historic weather patterns on a date selected and suggests which type of outerwear would be most suitable.

Clothing

First-off, choose your clothing style. Masculine, feminine, or a mix of both. Pair it with your age if you'd like to see something more relevant to you.

Destination & Date

Second, choose where you're headed, and when you're going. Traveling should be fun, you shouldn't have to overthink what you're packing.

Plan

Lastly, take a look at sample outerwear for where and when you're headed. With the historic weather forecast, you'll always be prepared for your next adventure.
